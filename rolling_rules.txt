## Stats & Rolls

**Attributes (1-5):** Determine talent (# of dice)
- **Physique**: Fitness, Deflection, Might, Endurance
- **Finesse**: Evasion, Stealth, Coordination, Thievery  
- **Mind**: Resilience, Concentration, Senses, Logic
- **Knowledge**: Wildcraft, Academics, Magic, Medicine
- **Social**: Expression, Presence, Insight, Persuasion

**Skills (0-6):** Determine die size
- 0=d4, 1=d6, 2=d8, 3=d10, 4=d12, 5=d16, 6=d20, 7=d24

**Rolling:** [Talent]d[Skill die], take highest
- 2 talent, 3 skill = 2d10 take highest
- Bonus dice: add more dice
- Penalty dice: reduce dice, then take lowest if <1

**Weapon Talents:** Unarmed, Throwing, Simple/Complex Melee/Ranged
**Magic Talents:** Black, Primal, Metamagic, Divine, Mysticism  
**Crafting Talents:** Engineering, Fabrication, Alchemy, Cooking, Glyphcraft, Bioshaping
